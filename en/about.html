About Sofie

20+ years of bringing people together through music

Music has a unique power to bring people together - to create energy, joy and community across all backgrounds. My speciality is gospel, a tradition that at its core is about exactly that: lifting each other up through song. I am passionate about creating those moments where the audience rises to their feet, the singers feel truly alive, and the music forges a genuine connection.

My Approach

As a conductor and producer, I build experiences from the ground up. My work ranges from developing the concept, assembling choir and band, coordinating dancers and managing the practical execution, to standing on stage and leading it all myself. Large-scale productions with professional musicians. Workshops where employees find community through song. Regardless of scale, I handle both the artistic and the practical.

I also teach conductors and choirs both in Denmark and internationally. From masterclasses at the Rhythmic Music Conservatory to workshops at international choir festivals - I share techniques, experience and a passion for choir leadership.

I believe everyone can sing - it's about finding the joy in music and letting yourself be swept up by the energy.

Experience That Counts

Over the years, I have led 14 different choirs from children to adults, and performed on stages from The Royal Theatre's Gamle Scene to Roskilde Festival, from Black churches in London's East End to concert halls in Vienna. I was the support act for Kirk Franklin at Forum, sang with multi-million selling Donald Lawrence, and conducted 1,000 singers at Gospel Aid Festival.

As a producer, I coordinated Denmark's largest flash mob with 500 singers at CPH Airport - from assembling 8 choirs to managing camera crews and sound engineers. For the DI Topmøde (Confederation of Danish Industry Summit), I served as creative director, production manager and soloist - building the show from concept to final note. I have led workshops for some of Denmark's largest companies and organisations - from Anklagemyndigheden with 1,200 participants to CPH Airport, DI Dansk Industri, Novo, Nordea, DSB and IBM.

But what I am most proud of are the thousands of singers I have had the pleasure of working with, and the countless moments where music has created a genuine connection between people.
